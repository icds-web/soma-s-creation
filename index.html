ABOUT

Based in Asansol, West Bengal, Soma's Creation was founded by its namesake, Soma Chatterjee, who is an approachable and enthusiastic entrepreneur balancing home and business like a rockstar.

Soma's Creation was founded in 2018 from one spare room in her house catering to just friends and family. In the last 2.5 years, it has grown to stunning heights with an online customer base of more than 30,000 people, shipping across India.

All this was possible not only through the perseverance of Ms Chatterjee, but also because of the unflinching support of initial and long-standing customers across India. The success of the business solely depends on our service, quality of our products and genuine prices.

HISTORY OF HANDLOOM

The earliest record of sari weaving in Bengal can be traced back to the 15th century in the Shantipur district. The art continued to flourish during the Mughal rule, from the 16th to the 18th centuries, when it received extensive royal patronage alongside muslin and jamdani.

After the partition of Bengal in 1947, several weavers from Bangladesh migrated to India and were rehabilitated in West Bengal. Phulia, close to Shantipur, became a new home for these weavers from Tangail (in Bangladesh), who also brought their ancestral weaving traditions with them.

“Every tant sari has a story to tell—it is representative of the finest and most ancient weaving technique. They are woven with locally procured Bengal cotton. This fine handspun yarn results in soft, feather-light muslin and mulmul textiles that have been globally traded for ages now".
